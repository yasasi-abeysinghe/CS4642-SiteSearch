Frequently Asked questions about Grocery Delivery in Sri Lanka through Kapruka

Where is your shipping center?

All grocery products will be shipped from Central London.

What shipping methods do you offer?

We offer Royal Mail Ground Shipping.

How do you calculate shipping charges?

Shipping will be calculated based on your order's weight. To get best rates you must order atleast 10 items.

How long does is usually take for my packages to arrive?

Most packages are shipped out the same day you place the order. Shipping time to most states take between 1 and 3 business days.

How do I track my orders?

You may use the Kapruka order tracking page or use your order tracking number directly on Royalmail package tracking site. Royalmail Ground Service 08457 740 740

Do you ship to PO Boxes?

No, we only ship to residential and business addresses.
