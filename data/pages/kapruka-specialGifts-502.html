Why shop at Kapruka for Sarees

Colours are all the rage these days; when Bollywood stars strut out in bright hues of magenta mixed with parrot green or deep shades of blue blending in to electrifying tones of orange, the whole world stops and stares, thus a fashion statement is born and adopted in all saree wearing regions. We at Kapruka feel these vibrant vibes that sway towards the latest trends and stock some of the most sought after designer sarees, wedding sarees and party sarees.


As much as there is an influence from neighbouring India and the glitzy world of Bollywood, Sri Lanka has its own style and focus when it comes to sarees. For instance handloom sarees in Sri Lanka are some of the most admired and widely worn garments nowadays. It is evident that these six yards of elegance are eternal, yet the handlooms outdo all the others in winning the hearts of the young and the old. Amidst the bold handlooms, the Kapruka range also includes silk, cotton and handmade sarees. We at Kapruka take a personal interest in each product we make available through our e-commerce site, hence whether the item is imported or produced locally we ensure that the highest standards are met in terms of quality, uniqueness, attractiveness and value for money.


Shop online for not just the trendiest sarees but also for a saree blouse that matches, a lehenga or a lehenga saree. We have a wide selection of colours to suit every mood and complexion, our styles and prints are modern and the range of textures suit a myriad of drapes from the fashionable Indian style to the conventional Kandyan drape and to the more contemporary butterfly or mermaid style wrap. Kapruka believes in making the online shopping experience as wonderful as a journey through the numerous floors and racks of a typical saree store, minus the hassle and the traveling, hence we take care of the delivery of your chosen items to literally anywhere in the country.

If ever there were a garment that brought out the best in a lady in the most sensuous yet dignified manner, it is the saree. From the desk at office to a wedding hall, from a meeting in the boardroom to a party, this all enduring garment remains dressy and classy. The uses are endless, the occasions are numerous, yet the online price that you would have to pay Kapruka for these exquisite lengths of woven fabric is probably less than what you would spend at an actual saree shop. Come discover the glamour of life in a saree or gift one and add some colour to someone's day.
