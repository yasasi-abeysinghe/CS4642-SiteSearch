Frequently Asked questions about Foodcity Grocery Delivery in Sri Lanka through Kapruka

Is the price same as store?

Yes - Absolutely. Kapruka's and Cargills Foodcity's main goal of this service is to deliver grocery to your door step at the same price as the store, so you save your time and money on the trips to grocery store.

Is there a delivery fee?

Colombo and immediate suburbs are free. Other areas delivery fees start from Rs 100. Kapruka will deliver your grocery to anywhere in the island. No area limitations

How fast can you deliver?

We deliver the same day within Colombo and immediate suburbs. Rest of the island is next day delivery.

How many items are available in the grocery section?

Almost all essential products at Cargills foodcity are available online. Perishable items are limited but will be increased in due time.

Can I order other Kapruka products with grocery items?

Yes- Certainly. You can mix any Kapruka products with your grocery order and we will deliver them together.

Can I re-order from order history?

Yes - Absolutely and this is very commonly used among our customers. Simply log into your Kapruka account and click the 'Re-Order' button on any of your old orders.
