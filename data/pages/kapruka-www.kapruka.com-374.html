Kapruka Online Shopping - Who we are

Kapruka.com is Sri Lanka's largest e-commerce organization. Send Gifts to Sri Lanka is one of Kapruka's primary services. Kapruka primary goal to provide a world class service to Sri Lankan's who shop online. Inspired by the truly Sri Lankan tradition Kapruka welcomes you for the experience of professional online gift delivery service in the international standards.


In addition to traditional e-commerce, Kapruka also pioneered a new online platform named Kapruka Global Shop which let's Sri Lanka's to order any item from global e-commerce giants such as Amazon, e-bay, Flipkart and Alibaba.
